Van Leasing with Insurance

Insurance, Maintenance, Roadside Assistance and More

Built to Suit Your Business

One of the most common questions we get asked is if insurance is included when leasing a van. For most leasing providers the answer is simple - no! However at vanleasing.com we can build monthly packages that include not only insurance but maintenance, servicing, roadside assistance and more. To build your bespoke leasing and insurance package, request a call back from one of our leasing advisors today.

Van Insurance

There's a lot of additional costs associated with motoring today; whether it's road tax, road fund license, insurance or even just the fuel, it can all add up. At vanleasing.com we don't just help you save money on the leasing part, we can also help with insuring the van. We search the market on your behalf to find the lowest cost fully comprehensive insurance on the market. To get started on your leasing journey, build your tailored online quote or open your online account.

Roadside Assistance

We only supply brand new vehicles, which means your chance of breakdown during your lease period is pretty sli. Having said that, for an extremely low-cost each month you can add roadside assistance to your lease deal - giving you the ultimate peace-of-mid that we've got you covered no matter what the eventuality.

Maintenance, Servicing, and Tyres


All of our lease vehicles come supplied with full manufacturers warranty as standard. To supplement this many of our customers decide to add on an additional maintenance and servicing package. It allows them to pay a low fixed monthly cost for all maintenance requirements for the vehicle over the lifetime of the lease. If your are doing a lot of mileage this can be a fantastic way to ensure there are no unexpected large bills for tyres and servicing during the lease period.
