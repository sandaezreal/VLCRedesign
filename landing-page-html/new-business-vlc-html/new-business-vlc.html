Lightning-quick van leasing approval

We want to make it easier for new businesses to reap the benefits of affordable van finance so we've simplified our process to let you find out in an instant if you’re approved. Just click ‘Approve Me’ to find out if you’re eligible for new business van leasing.

We work with businesses of all sizes; from recently setup sole traders and limited companies, through to multi-million pound organisations running large fleets of vehicles. In every case we work hard to find a van and leasing package that’s well suited to your needs and represents fantastic value. Our aim is to make the process of leasing a new van faster, simpler and less-hassle. These are just a few of the ways we like make your life easier:

1. Almost Unlimited Choice

We’re here to help you find the van that’s most suited to your business, whether that be a Ford, Volkswagen, Renault, Vauxhall or any other manufacturer. Our buying power and access to every make and model of light commercial vehicle in the UK gives us the ability to work with you to select a new van from a huge range of potential options. All vans are available on flexible and low-cost leasing packages tailored to your business.



2. Free nationwide delivery

Wherever you are in the mainland UK we deliver your new van to your door completely free of any delivery charges. With thousands of vehicles in stock and ready for immediate delivery, we can have your van at your front door in just 7 days. All of our vans come with the full manufacturers warranty and the option to add on low-cost maintenance packages that cover every eventuality for the duration of your lease – including tyres and servicing.



3. Do it all online

One of the most frustrating aspects of leasing a new van for your business can be getting a real idea of how much it is going to cost each month. Most leasing websites are flooded with illustrative ‘from’ prices that often don’t have much relation with what you end up paying, particularly if you have a higher mileage and don’t want to pay several thousands pounds upfront. Our online quote generator is unique in the marketplace – giving you real quotations based on your vehicle choice, annual mileage, lease length and other factors to determine an exact online quotation. You can generate as many of these quotes as you want until you find a vehicle and price that suits your budget. It’s not just the online quotation that we’ve brought online; our simple approval and application tool allow you to make a leasing application in just a matter of minutes. It’s fast, easy to use and can even be completed on your tablet or mobile device.




4. No dreaded call centres

We may give you all of the tools to generate quotes and complete your application online but we also believe in the importance of good old-fashioned customer service. Our highly trained leasing experts are here to help and ready for your call. We don’t have huge call centres, automated call systems and long waits to speak to someone –we answer your call within three rings and go out of our way to provide you with a positive experience. If you choose to lease a van with us you are assigned a dedicated leasing advisor who will deal with your case from initial enquiry, through the delivery of your new van and the whole duration of your lease.




5. New businesses welcome

Many leasing companies are unable or unwilling to deal with newly incorporated limited companies or self-employed sole traders. At vanleasing.com we take a different approach; helping new start companies to get off the ground with a cash flow friendly and affordable leasing solution tailored to your budget. Get in touch today to find out how we can help find a brand new van for your business.
