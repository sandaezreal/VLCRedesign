Van leasing – not just for corporations

There's a lot of additional costs associated with motoring today; whether it's road tax, road fund license, insurance or even just the fuel, it can all add up. At vanleasing.com we don't just help you save money on the leasing part, we can also help with insuring the van. We search the market on your behalf to find the lowest cost fully comprehensive insurance on the market. To get started on your leasing journey, build your tailored online quote or open your online account.

Personal Van Leasing

Personal van leasing gives you the freedom of driving the vehicle as if it were your own for a low initial deposit followed by agreed monthly payments for the duration of the lease. At vanleasing.com we’re really flexible and we don’t mind adjusting the monthly payments to work with your own finances. Other finance options include lease purchase (if you want to buy the vehicle from us at the end of the lease) and contract hire. With a contract hire deal you can bundle in other packages with you van for a hassle free, fixed monthly payment.



Any make and model, from panel vans to minibuses


From Ford Fiestas to long wheelbase Mercedes Sprinters we’re able to source all new van makes and models available in the UK today. Panel vans are a popular choice with workers who need to be able to carry the tools of their trade as they’re tougher, more practical and more secure than a car but your van doesn’t have to be spec’d for loading goods and equipment. If it’s people you need to carry around we have lots of minibuses and crew vans to choose from; the benefits here being the extra space you might need but often with the creature comforts of a car you’re already used to.
