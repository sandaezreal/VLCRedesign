About Us

Get to know VanLeasing.com

Always Here to Assist

Did you know that we’ve been leasing commercial vans to customers all over the UK since 1992 and have been trading online for over 15 years? A lot’s changed in that time, not least the vehicles we lease, which have gone from rather boxy and unexciting looking workhorses that existed in 1992 right through to some of the stunning new and featured packed vehicles that we see on our roads today. From Mercedes, and Volkswagens through to Fords, Citroens and Ivecos, every single manufacturer now has something unique and exciting to offer the UK’s business owners.

It’s our job to help you look at the features of each van and the different finance options available to help you pick out the best combination to meet your needs. We work with businesses all over the UK and our team of leasing experts are here to help you find the best deal today.

Customer Service

1. We’re committed to providing fantastic customer service for every single one of our customers and always strive to meet or beat your expectations. Our pledge to you includes:

2. All calls answered by a human being within 30-seconds! That means no automated call processes and no long wait to speak to someone.

A Simple Process

At vanleasing.com we want to make the process of selecting and leasing a new van as simple as possible. Almost every step of the process is available to complete online – from the initial quote through to opening an account and completing your application to lease a van with us. We’re also on-hand on the phone to help you through the process and offer expert guidance and help on both vehicle types and the finance options available to you as a business.
